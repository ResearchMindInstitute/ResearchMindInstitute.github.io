Philosophy


Process of Knowledge and Impact

Understanding intellectual content is greatly unlike memorizing the material studied. Unlike aimless memorization and repetition, not only does it aid long-term memory, it instills a greater value to learning and provides motivation for people to seek insight and personal improvement after the set stages of education. Understanding allows derivation and development to knowledge once memorized. Worth and value attributed to learning leads to enhanced depth and understanding to the material, bringing classroom information to a higher, more complex level found in the professional field.
