A Student Reflection Essay

On the 2024 Winter Camp


Last week I attended the 2024 Winter Camp. It was a very precious time where I learned many teamwork skills, explored how to question and think critically, and discovered more facts about the renowned Abraham Lincoln's life, occupation, and presidency. It was really interesting to challenge questions regarding cases Abraham might have faced as a lawyer and how books and many difficulties shaped him. It helped me get an understanding of the difficulties of being a lawyer and how important the influence of books are.

On the first day, we discussed the influence of books in Abraham Lincoln's life. Reading many books is important, but it's not about how fast or how many books you read, but how well you understand it. You need to practice what you have learned, and then apply it to our lives. How foolish would it be to hold on to important teachings? Even if you learn important things, if you do not practice and apply it, it is useless.

Books are important as they are foundations to our lives. Each book has a message the author is trying to relay and you must find it and use it as a tool for decisions and facing difficulties. Your reading strategies may be different from others and unusual. However, the pace does not matter, you must be able to understand what you are reading. Also, you must have the right mindset of eagerness to learn and experience new things.

On the second day, we answered cases that may be presented to a lawyer. You may think that being a lawyer is easy, but in reality there are many challenges to it, especially back in the 1800s where there was a weaker government and not many established laws. The cases presented to us were a great example of how difficult it is to prosecute as it is to defend. Also, it showed that the outcome of cases can vary widely because there are many different state laws, even on the same subject. Also being a lawyer would not be easy as there may be more favorable positions to argue for and there may be many assumptions made.

In conclusion, this 2024 Winter Camp was such a very valuable experience where I learned many teamwork skills, explored how to question and think critically, and discovered more facts about the renowned Abraham Lincoln's life, occupation, and presidency. It was really interesting to challenge questions regarding cases Abraham might have faced as a lawyer and how books and many difficulties shaped him. This camp was very fun and exciting and I would like to do this again in the future.
