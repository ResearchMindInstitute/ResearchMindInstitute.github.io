Book Club


Research Mind Book Club

The book club's purpose is to create a passage of maturity in the reading journeys of students. Inspired by the idea extended by How to Read a Book, Research Mind Book Club drives participants to read challenging but beneficial books and reflect on them through student discussions. Meetings based on assigned reading gives participants the motivation to closely read material outside their usual comfort zone
